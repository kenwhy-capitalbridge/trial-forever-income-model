
CAPITAL BRIDGE - FOREVER INCOME CALCULATOR KNOWLEDGE BASE

1. MISSION STATEMENT
The Forever Income Calculator is a high-level wealth analytics tool designed to determine the "Sustainability Threshold." It calculates the exact amount of capital required to fund a specific lifestyle indefinitely without ever depleting the principal sum, accounting for the erosive effects of inflation.

2. CORE METHODOLOGY (THE MATH)
The calculator utilizes the "Real Return Rate" model.
- Formula: Capital Required = Annual Expenses / (Expected ROI % - Inflation Rate %)
- Logic: To live off capital forever, you must only spend the portion of your returns that exceeds inflation. If you spend the full ROI, your purchasing power will decrease every year.
- Sustainability Rule: If (Expected ROI - Inflation) is zero or negative, the strategy is marked as "Negative Yield" (unsustainable), as the capital will eventually run out regardless of the starting amount.

3. KEY INPUT DATA POINTS
- Lifestyle Spend: The target annual or monthly cash flow required for all living expenses.
- Capital Allocation:
    a) Liquid Assets: Cash, savings, and immediate salary.
    b) Investments: Stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or private equity.
    c) Property/Company/Others: Tangible assets or business valuations.
- Market Expectations:
    - Expected ROI: The anticipated annual growth of the portfolio.
    - Inflation Rate: The expected long-term increase in the cost of living (typically 3-4%).

4. INTERPRETING RESULTS
- Target Fund: The total capital required to reach "Perpetual Income" status.
- Current Wealth: The sum of all current assets provided by the user.
- Funding Gap: The remaining capital needed to achieve the goal. If zero, the user is "Fully Funded."
- Independence Bridge (Progress %): A visual representation of how close the user is to their target.
- Annual Multiplier: Calculated as (Capital Needed / Annual Expense). This shows how many "years" of expenses are required in the fund to sustain the lifestyle forever (e.g., a 25x multiplier is common for a 4% real return).
